Choose Your Home Site First

The size, shape, slope, and contour of your land directly affect your home’s layout, foundation, and cost. We strongly recommend choosing your location before purchasing blueprints.

We’ll meet you on your property anywhere in Cumberland, Harnett, or North West Sampson County to help determine the best placement.
